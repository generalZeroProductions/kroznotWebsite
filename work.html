ITEC Europe

France

The International Industry Technology Trade and Exhibition Centre, located in the heart of Europe, is the largest building of it's kind in Europe. In developing their market on the Chinese mainland, they approached us to create this comprehensive promotional video. The work entailed recreating the center in 3D space and rendering out numerous scenes of the bustling mega centre, as well as assisting them in localizing the marketing message for China.

Shui Jin Street

Cheng Du

When the Shui Jin Street shopping center in Cheng Du, Si Chuan province, was nearly finished with construction, they called us in to get the brand and the facility ready for operation. We worked closely with them for nearly six months developing a complete visual identity system and creating a one-year calendar of events. This short video was used to present the soon-to-be-completed center to potential tenants.
