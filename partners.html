International Coucil of Shopping Centers

Founded in 1957, ICSC is the global trade association of the shopping center industry. Its more than 70,000 members in over 100 countries include shopping center owners, developers, managers, investors, retailers, brokers, academics, and public officials.

In 2011 when ICSC began their entrance into the Chinese market, Kroznot was selected as the official marketing and communications partner. Since then we have worked closely with them to organize numerous conferences and other events, develop and maintain their Chinese website, and facilitate many other aspects of their successful ongoing operations here.
