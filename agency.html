Why should impossible get in the way?

Our story began in 2006 when a young American bet everything on his passions and a dream. Undercapitalized, new to China, and sorely lacking in business acumen, his chances of success were little better than those of Sisyphus and the challenges were equally arduous. But a willingness to try when there is no reason to believe in success, well, isn't that what makes a story worth telling?

Based on his love of digital media and web technologies, he set out to create a visual effects house (hence the quirky name, Kroznot) but by the second year it was clear that the Chinese market was most in need of an original and affordable brand agency. The basics of branding were still new to many mainland enterprises and the primary houses in Beijing were all ultra-expensive, 4A agencies.

The decision was tough but clear, and we set about retooling, by our fifth year we were serving some of the most prestigious commercial centers in the city. Years later we continue to add to our portfolio, having represented, China World Mall, Sundainty, Joy City, Lu Neng and many other top-tier brands. You might think we've done everything there is to do, but we know we're just getting started.



Getting Down to Brass Tacks

A company's culture is tantamount to the culture of a nation. If you want your people to go to war for you, you'd better give them something to believe in. In our company that has always been an obsessive attention to detail.

This may seem insignificant, but that is exactly the point. For any given project, there are myriad details we could skip over - but we never do. We are passionate about creating great brands and we know that the devil is in the details. We may work long nights, agonizing over the seemingly insignificant, but the agony turns to ecstasy when we see a client's reaction and we know we got it right.



Klan Kroznot

From early on our motto has been, "If you're not having fun, you're in the wrong place." We may be a band of misfits, but we all fit in here.

Spontaneous, fun-loving, creative, hard-working, these are a few of the best adjectives to describe the amazing group of people that have to come to work at Kroznot. We pride ourselves on our casual-but-cordial atmosphere and we have been incredibly fortunate to have almost no turn over (several of our best people have been with us for more than five years.)

The management and staff of any company are crucial, but this is especially so in the service industry. We place enormous emphasis on creating a culture where intelligent and motivated people can thrive and excell and we are ever so careful to hire only those who we know will bring a spark to our office and the work we do for our clients.
